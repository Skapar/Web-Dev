Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredient Quantity
Flour2 cups
Sugar1 cup
Chocolate Chips1 cup

Preparation Steps:

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2: Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

Step 3: Place dough on baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
